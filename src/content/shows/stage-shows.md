*An Evening with Victor Ashveil* is a full-length production that has played to sold-out audiences across the country. Over ninety minutes, Victor takes his audience through a journey of psychological illusion, genuine connection, and carefully constructed wonder — exploring what it means to believe, to doubt, and to be surprised by the limits of your own perception.

The show is produced for theatre environments but has been adapted for festival stages and unusual venues. Technical requirements are detailed in the rider. Victor works directly with venue technical teams to ensure the production maintains its atmosphere in any space.
