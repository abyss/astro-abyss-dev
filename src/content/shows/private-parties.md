A private celebration calls for something personal. Victor performs close-up mentalism that moves through your guests during drinks — reading minds at arm's length, leaving small groups genuinely astonished — before gathering everyone for an intimate parlour show that feels like theatre performed just for your party.

These evenings tend to become the story your guests tell for years. The experience is tailored around your guests, with material that can incorporate details you choose to share in advance, creating moments that feel inexplicably personal.
