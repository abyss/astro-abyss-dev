Remote performance requires a fundamentally different approach. Victor's virtual show is not a stage show filmed on a webcam — it has been designed from the ground up for the platform, exploiting the unique properties of video calls to create effects that simply could not happen in a room.

Participants receive instructions in advance that seem innocuous but become meaningful during the show. The experience is interactive throughout, with genuine participation from attendees regardless of their location. Consistently rated as the most memorable element of virtual company events.
